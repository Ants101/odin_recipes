Toasted Garlic Bread Recipe

Enjoy with any great meal or you could even enjoy it by itself.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) loaf italian bread
  • 5 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prehet the broiler.
  2. Cut the bread into slices 1 to 2 inches thick.
  3. In a small bowl, mix butter, olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the mixture evenly on the bread slices.
  5. On a medium baking sheet, arrange the slices evenly and broil 5 minutes, or until slightly brown. Check frequently so they do not burn.
  6. Remove from broiler. Top with cheese and return to broiler for 2 to 3 minutes, until cheese is slightly brown and melted. Serve at once.
