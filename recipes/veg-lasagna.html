Vegetable Lasagna

This is a lasagna that both vegetarians and non-vegetrians can eat and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 15 uncooked oven-ready (no boil) lasagna noodles
  • 1 can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 package frozen spinach, thawed and well drained
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the broth in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat to boil.
  2. Break noodles into pieces and add to the broth.
  3. Reduce the heat to low. Cook for 3 minutes or until the noodles are tender.
  4. Stir the soup, tomatoes and spinach in the skillet. Cook for 5 minutes or until the mixture is hot and bubbling.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat. Spoon the ricotta cheese on top and sprinkle with the mozzarella cheese.
